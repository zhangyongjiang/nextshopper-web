Merchant FAQ

How to open a store?

  • Sign up an account
  • Complete account and store settings
  • Upload your product

What's cost to open store?

It's FREE! There is no monthly fees or any upfront fees.

What's NextShopper commission?

No sales no fee! You only need to pay when you have a sale. NextShopper will charge 15% of total order.

Can I charge shipping fee?

No, for a better user experience, we require merchant to provide free shipping on their listing.

How to fulfill the order?

You will receive alert as well as email notification when there is new order. Update shipping information on merchant site and ship out the order ASAP.

What's return & refund policy?

Merchant at NextShopper need to accept returns within 15 days after delivery. If refund is required, you need to issue refund within 2 days.

How do I get paid?

The minimum payout at NextShopper is $200 USD.

Account monthly statement date is on 24th day of each month. Merchant will be paid on 1st day of the following month. Funds will be sent on 1st day of each month except weekend and Holiday (US). In case of weekend or holiday (US), Funds will be sent on next business day (Monday - Friday).In order to be paid, your account available balance need to have at least 200 US dollars on statement day. Funds will be skipped from current payment cycle until available balance reach 200 US dollars. Funds will be automatically transfer to your bank account. At this moment, we only support auto transfer to bank in USA and P.R China. There is $1.50 processing fee for each payment, in addition, we charge 1.5% of total amount if you want to receive RMB with bank in China.
