FAQ

Cancellation

Q: How do I cancel my order?

A: If something comes up and you need to cancel your order, contact seller from your order page to request cancellation.
Note: Accepting your request is up to the seller. Submitting a cancellation request does not automatically cancel your order.

Payment

Q: How nextshopper protects my payments?

A: We never keep your payment information, the transaction is protected by VeriSign SSL encryption (the highest level of protection commercially available), so you are safe with us.

Q: Does Nextshopper charge for customs, taxes, VAT, or any other extra fees?

A: Nextshopper only charges for purchase price and shipping rate. We do not collect any payment for customs, taxes, VAT, or any other fees.

Return and Refund

Q: What should I do if the items I received were not as described by the seller?

A: Any product delivered to you that fails to match the seller’s description is eligible for a full refund. You can also work out a solution that is agreeable to both you and the seller.

Q: How can I ensure the products I purchase are genuine?

A: All sellers are guarantee that the products they sell on nextshopper.com are genuine. If you find anything wrong, please contact us, and we will ensure you get your money back.

Q: What if I am simply unhappy with my purchase? Can I return it, even if it is in perfect condition and my order hasn’t been completed?

A: Yes, you can return the item within 15 days after you receive the product. Item must be unused with original packing.

Shipping

Q: What can I do if my order doesn't arrive within the seller's guaranteed delivery time?

A: All products on nextshopper are guaranteed to be delivered within 60 days. You are protected and get full refund if you don't receive products within the time.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: Go to your order history page, and you will see tracking information on order detail page.
