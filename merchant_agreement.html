Merchant Policies

General practice

  • Do not list items on NextShopper if you do not own or have permission for the trademark, copyright or patent;

  • You may not purchase your own products through the NextShopper platform;
  • You may not review your own item;
  • You may not use the NextShopper brand to promote your listings.

Prohibited items

The following types of items may not be listed on Nextshopper: 

  • Counterfeit products
  • Items without own or hold copyright;
  • Digital products that are not tangible;
  • Any type of giftcard;
  • Alcohols;
  • Tobacco and other smokeable products including electronic cigarettes.
  • Lighters
  • Drugs,medical drug claims about an item, drug paraphernalia
  • Live animals, illegal animal products
  • Human remains or body parts (excluding hair and teeth)
  • Pornography or adult/sexually explicit/obscene material
  • Firearms and/or weapons
  • Items or listings that promote, support or glorify hatred toward or otherwise demean people based upon: race, ethnicity, religion, gender, gender identity, disability, or sexual orientation; including items or content that promote organizations with such views

We reserve the right to remove products that we determine are not suitable to nextshopper users.

Listings

  • You must be the copyright holder or licensed to sell the products you listed.
  • All listings on NextShopper must be for a tangible object only.
  • All listing must not be conditional upon the purchase of another listing in your store
  • All listing must be free delivery.
  • You cannot use NextShopper to direct shoppers to online selling venue another than NextShopper itself;
  • Product photo must be clear without any watermarks;
  • Description must be accurate;

Listings that do not comply with Nextshopper's policies may be removed from or suspended on Nextshopper and your account may be suspended or terminated.

Eligible to Pay

In order to get paid, you need to complete all 3 following items;

  • Add valid tracking # on order page.

Fee

Nextshopper charge 15% commission for total sales on NextShopper and $1.50 USD for each paper check sent to merchant.


Payment

NextShopper use “ +3 “ and “ +30 “ method to settle the payment with merchant.

Order will not be paid if there is no tracking number.

  • If tracking number can be verified,orders will be eligible to pay 3 days from merchant submitted tracking number.
  • If tracking number is invalid or can’t be verified, orders will be eligible to pay after 30 days from merchant submitted tracking number.
Nextshopper will send payment monthly with minimum $200.
  • Account monthly statement date is on 24th day of each month. Merchant will be paid on 1st day of the following month.
  • In order to be paid, your account available balance must have 200 US dollars or more on statement day.
  • Your payment will be skipped from current payment cycle until available balance reach 200 US dollars.
  • Payment will be sent on 1st day of each month except weekend and Holiday(US). In case of weekend or holiday (US), payment will be sent on next business day (Monday - Friday).
  • There is payment processing fee$1.50, it will deduct from your payment.
  • All payment will send via paper check, you will receive payment in 5-8 days via USPS in most of US addresses; 14-21 days for international addresses.
