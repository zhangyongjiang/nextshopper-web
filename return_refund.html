Return & Refund Policy

Nextshopper is a marketplace that promise 100% buyer satisfaction. In order to protect our buyers, Nextshopper requires sellers to accept returns. You may return the item within 15 days after you receive the product, your item must be in its original unused condition, unless there is a manufacturer defect, fake or fails to match the product description.

It may take 7 business days for a return to fully complete. Refunds are issued based on the payment method used at the time of purchase. And you should receive your refund within 7 business days after return is complete.

Return Exceptions

Some items cannot be returned if they are opened. These include digital media, Perfumes/ Fragrances and underwear/swimming wear.

Merchandise that has been worn, used, or altered will not be accepted for return or exchange.

Please do not return any item to the seller without the consent of the seller.

Buyer will pay for the cost of return shipping.

Seller and nextshopper does reserve the right to deny refunds for items that are not returned in the same condition they were received

Although we do our best to facilitate a unique and friendly experience for all users, we would like to stress our encouragement of positive interaction amongst members of our community. Should a dispute arise, please contact us.
