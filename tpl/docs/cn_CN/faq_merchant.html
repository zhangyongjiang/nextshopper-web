常见问题

How to open a store?

  • Sign up an account;
  • Complete account and store information and make sure it's accurate;
  • Upload your product to sale

What's cost to open store?

It's FREE! There is no monthly fees or any upfront fees, When you have a sale on nextshopper, Nextshopper will change 15% commission of your total orders.

Can I charge shipping fee?

No, for better customer experience, we require all listing with free shipping. Most of our customer are in North America, please consider your shipping cost when you set the listing/sale price.

How to fufill the order?

You will receive alert as well as email notification when there is new order. Update shipping information on merchant site and shipping out the item ASAP.

What's return & refund policy?

NextShopper merchant must accpet returns with 15 days after items are shipped. If refund is required, you need to issue refund within 48 hours.

How do I get paid?

NextShopper will pay you on first day of every month. In case of weekend or Holiday, you payment will be sent on next business day. All payment will be sent via paper check, you will receive payment in 5-8 days via USPS in most of US addresses; 14-21 days for international addresses. There is payment processing fee$1.50, it will deduct from your payment.
