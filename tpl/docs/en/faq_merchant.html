Merchant FAQ

How to open a store?

  • Sign up an account;
  • Complete account and store settings;
  • Upload your product to sale.

What's cost to open store?

It's FREE! There is no monthly fees or any upfront fees.

What's NextShopper commission?

No sales no fee! You only need to pay when you have a sale. NextShopper will charge 15% of total order.

Can I charge shipping fee?

No, for a better user experience, we require merchant to provide free shipping on their listing.

How to fufill the order?

You will receive alert as well as email notification when there is new order. Update shipping information on merchant site and ship out the order ASAP.

What's return & refund policy?

Merchant at NextShopper must accept returns within 15 days after delivery. If refund is required, you need to issue refund within 2 days.

How do I get paid?

The minimum payout at NextShopper is $200 USD.

NextShopper statement day is 24th day of the month, and payout on first day of following month. In case of weekend or Holiday, you payment will be sent on next business day. All payment will be sent via paper check, you will receive payment in 5-8 days via USPS in most of US addresses; 14-21 days for international addresses. There is payment processing fee$1.50, it will be deducted from your payment.
