Merchant FAQ (English)

How will I know if there is a new order?

Nextshopper will send notification email when you have new order, you can also check orders from merchant site. You will see new order notification on the navigation tab.

Can I charge shipping fee?

No, for better customer experience, we require all listing with free shipping. Most of our customer are in North America, please consider your shipping cost when you set the listing/sale price.

How do I get paid?

Nextshopper will send payment monthly with minimum $200.
  • Account monthly statement date is on 24th day of each month. Merchant will be paid on 1st day of the following month.
  • In order to be paid, your account available balance must have 200 US dollars or more on statement day.
  • Your payment will be skipped from current payment cycle until available balance reach 200 US dollars.
  • Payment will be sent on 1st day of each month except weekend and Holiday (US). In case of weekend or holiday (US), payment will be sent on next business day (Monday - Friday).
  • There is payment processing fee$1.50, it will deduct from your payment.
  • All payment will send via paper check, you will receive payment in 5-8 days via USPS in most of US addresses; 14-21 days for international addresses.

When is my order eligible to pay?

NextShopper use “ +3 “ and “ +30 “ method to settle the payment with merchant.

Order will not be paid if there is no tracking inforamtion.

  • If tracking number can be verified,orders will be eligible to pay 3 days after merchant submitted tracking number.
  • If tracking number is invalid or can’t be verified, orders will be eligible to pay 30 days after merchant submitted tracking number.

What's eligible to pay?

In order to get paid, you need to complete all 3 followings
  • Fulfill the open order.
  • Add valid tracking # on order page.
  • Complete all above within five (5) days.

What’s nextshopper fees?

Commission

There is no monthly fees or any upfront fees, When you have a sale on nextshopper, Nextshopper will change 15% commission of your total orders. No sales no fee!

Payment processing fee

Nextshopper will charge $1.50 for each payment we send to you.
